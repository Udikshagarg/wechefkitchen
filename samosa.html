SAMOSA RECEPIES

            

        For the Dough:

      1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
      2. 1/4 cup oil
      3. Water (as needed)
      4. Salt (to taste)
    
       For the Filling:

       1. 2-3 boiled potatoes, peeled and mashed
       2. 1 cup boiled peas
       3. 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
       4. 1 teaspoon grated ginger
       5. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
       6. 1 teaspoon garam masala
       7. 1 teaspoon coriander powder
       8. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
       9. Salt (to taste)
       10. Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
       11. Oil for frying 

       Instructions:
          For the Dough:

       1. In a mixing bowl, add all-purpose flour, salt, and oil. Mix well until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
        2. Gradually add water and knead to form a firm, smooth dough. Cover it with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.

       For the Filling:

      1. Heat a little oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
      2. Add grated ginger and chopped green chilies. Saut√© for a minute.
      3. Add boiled peas and mashed potatoes. Mix well.
      4. Add garam masala, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
      5. Stir in the chopped coriander leaves and remove from heat. Allow the mixture to cool.

      For Assembling and Frying:

     1.Divide the dough into equal portions and shape them into balls. Roll each ball into a thin oval or circle.
     2. Cut the rolled dough in half to form two semi-circles.
     3. Take one semi-circle, apply water along the straight edge, and fold it to form a cone shape.
     4. Fill the cone with the prepared potato-peas mixture. Seal the open edge using a little water to make a triangular pocket.
     5. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
     6. Carefully slide the samosas into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
     7. Remove the fried samosas and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
     8. Serve hot with mint chutney, tamarind chutney, or ketchup.

                                             Enjoy your homemade samosas!
